Binary Search Tree
A data structure where each node has up to 2 child nodes. The left child must be smaller than the parent, and the right node must be larger.
Stack
A stack is a data structure where items can be pushed onto the stack or popped off. It is like a stack of books because you can only pop off the most recent item, and when you push onto the stack, you add it on top. This is known as last on, first off ordering.
Queue
Similar to the stack, a queue is first in, first out ordering. You put something into the queue, and it will be the very next thing when you take an item out of the queue. This is similar to a line of people waiting to go on an amusement park ride.
